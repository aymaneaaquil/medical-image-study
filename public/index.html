Medical Image Study

Developed by Aymane Aaquil, Alex, Abdel and Cameron Shortt
Under supervision of Dr. Yaser Esmaeili Salehani and Soorena Salari

This study explores the use of Stable Diffusion to augment skin cancer image datasets by generating high-quality synthetic dermoscopic images. The goal is to improve the performance of machine learning models in skin lesion classification by increasing data diversity without requiring extensive manual annotation.
